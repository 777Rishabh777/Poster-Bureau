Packaging & Delivery

Rigid Tube Packaging

All posters are rolled and shipped in hard cardboard tubes to prevent damage.

Pan-India Delivery

We ship to all pin codes across India via trusted courier partners.

Free Shipping

Orders above ₹499 get free standard shipping. Express delivery available at extra cost.

Tracking

Real-time tracking link sent via SMS and email once your order ships.

Cancellation Policy

Before Printing: Orders can be cancelled for a full refund within 2 hours of placing the order, provided the printing process hasn't begun.

After Printing: Since all posters are made-to-order, cancellations after printing begins are not possible. We'll reach out to find a solution if needed.

Refund Timeline: Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

Returns & Exchanges

We accept returns for damaged or defective products only. Please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage.

We'll send a free replacement or issue a full refund — your choice. Bureau covers return shipping for defective items.

For exchanges on undamaged items (size, design swap), a re-shipping fee may apply.
