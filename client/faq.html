FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions.

Quick answers to the most common questions about Bureau.

What paper quality do you use?

All posters are printed on 300GSM museum-grade matte paper with archival inks. This ensures vibrant colors, zero glare, and a premium feel that lasts 100+ years without fading.

How long does shipping take?

Orders are printed within 1-2 business days and shipped via premium courier. Total delivery takes 5-7 business days across India. You'll receive a tracking link via SMS and email.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel within 2 hours of placing the order (before printing starts). After that, cancellations aren't possible since prints are made-to-order. See our cancellation policy for details.

Do you offer international shipping?

Currently we ship only within India. International shipping is coming soon! Follow us on Instagram for updates.

What is the NFC chip on posters?

Every Bureau poster includes a small NFC tag that verifies authenticity. Tap your phone to the tag to view the poster's details, certificate of authenticity, and the artist info.

What sizes are available?

We currently offer A3 (11.7" × 16.5") and A2 (16.5" × 23.4") sizes. Custom sizes may be available on request — contact us for details.

How do I return a damaged poster?

Contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage. We'll send a free replacement or process a full refund — your choice. Bureau covers return shipping for defective items.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm), credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay), net banking, and digital wallets. All payments are processed securely.

Still have questions?

We're happy to help. Reach out and we'll get back within 24 hours.
